About Us

Company Overview

AVIRAN GLOBAL is a B2B export partner connecting global buyers to high-quality Indian products through structured sourcing, compliance, and dependable logistics.

Mission

To deliver trusted Indian export products with transparent communication, consistent quality, and long-term trade value for global buyers.

Vision

To be recognized as a reliable international trade partner for premium Indian coffee, cardamom, handloom products, and dehydrated foods.

Founder Message

Building trust through quality and execution.

We started AVIRAN GLOBAL to simplify sourcing from India for serious importers. Our commitment is straightforward: clear communication, verified quality, and reliable shipment handling from first conversation to final delivery.

We value lasting business relationships and support every buyer with practical solutions tailored to their product and market needs.

Core Values

The principles that guide every transaction.

Integrity

Transparent commitments and honest updates.

Quality

Inspection-first approach from sourcing to dispatch.

Reliability

Consistency in timelines, quality, and support.

Partnership

Long-term buyer success over one-time transactions.
